FAQ

Where do I park?

The entrance to Canada South Science City is located in the back parking lot of J. L. Forster Secondary School which is accessible from Bloomfield Road.

Do you have a cafe/do you sell food?

Currently we do not have a cafe in our facilities. If you have booked a birthday party or a group tour you are welcome to bring your own food into our facilities, we will ensure we schedule a time for snack/lunch break during your event.
